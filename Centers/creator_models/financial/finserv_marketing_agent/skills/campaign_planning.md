# Skill: Campaign Planning

## Skill ID
`finserv.skills.campaign_planning`

## Description
Governs the end-to-end planning of integrated marketing campaigns for financial services brands.

---

## Campaign Architecture

Every campaign is built on four pillars: Objective, Audience, Message, Experience.

---

## Campaign Brief Template

```
CAMPAIGN BRIEF
Version: 1.0 | Date: [YYYY-MM-DD] | Status: Draft

1. CAMPAIGN NAME
2. BUSINESS OBJECTIVE
   Target: [Specific, measurable goal]
3. MARKETING OBJECTIVE
   Funnel Stage: Awareness / Consideration / Conversion / Retention
   KPI: [Primary metric]
4. AUDIENCE
   Primary Segment: [Name + description]
   Secondary Segment: [Name + description]
   Audience Size Estimate: [Range]
   Exclusions: [Suppression lists]
5. KEY DATES
   Campaign Start / End / Creative Deadline / Compliance Deadline / Go-No-Go
6. BUDGET
   Total: $[Amount]
   Paid Media: $[Amount] ([%])
   Production: $[Amount] ([%])
   Reserve: $[Amount] ([%])
7. MESSAGING HIERARCHY
   Primary Message: [1 sentence]
   Supporting Messages: [2-3 proof points]
   Differentiator: [What makes this unique]
   CTA: [Verb + outcome]
8. CHANNEL PLAN
   | Channel | Role | Budget % | Format | KPI |
9. CREATIVE REQUIREMENTS
   Assets Needed / Brand Elements / Legal Elements
10. MEASUREMENT PLAN
    Primary KPI / Secondary KPIs / Cadence / Attribution Model
11. COMPLIANCE AND LEGAL
    Classification / Disclosures / Pre-clearance Required / Contacts
12. DEPENDENCIES AND RISKS
13. APPROVALS REQUIRED
    | Approver | Role | Due Date |
```

---

## Campaign Taxonomy

**By Objective:** Brand Awareness / Product Launch / Lead Generation / Acquisition / Cross-sell / Retention / Winback / Event

**By Product:** Banking / Investment / Insurance / Wealth Management / Commercial / Fintech

**By Duration:** Always-On / Seasonal / Campaign Flight / Event-Triggered

---

## Channel Strategy Rules

- Never launch paid financial product advertising without compliance pre-clearance
- Retargeting audiences must exclude suppression lists
- All email must comply with CAN-SPAM / CASL
- Suppression lists applied before every send
- Investment-adjacent content requires E-E-A-T signals and author credentials

| Objective | Paid Media | Content/Creative | Tech/Ops |
|---|---|---|---|

## Success Metrics by Campaign Type

| Campaign Type | Primary KPI | Secondary KPIs |
|---|---|---|
| Brand Awareness | Unaided awareness lift | Reach, frequency, brand search volume |
| Lead Generation | Qualified leads | CPL, lead-to-opportunity rate |
| Product Launch | Application starts | Page visits, CTR, activation rate |
| Retention | Churn rate reduction | NPS, product depth, engagement rate |
| Cross-sell | Products per household | Offer click rate, conversion rate |
