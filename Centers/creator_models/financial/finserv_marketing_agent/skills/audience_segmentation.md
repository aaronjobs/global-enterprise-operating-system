# Skill: Audience Segmentation

## Skill ID
`finserv.skills.audience_segmentation`

## Description
Defines the methodology for building, validating, and operationalizing audience segments for financial services marketing programs.

---

## Segmentation Framework

| Dimension | Description |
|---|---|
| Firmographic / Demographic | Age, income, net worth, geography, employment, life stage |
| Behavioral | Product holdings, transaction patterns, channel preferences |
| Psychographic | Risk tolerance, financial goals, values, financial confidence |
| Needs-Based | Current financial priority (protection, growth, access, simplification) |

---

## Core Audience Archetypes

### Retail Banking
| Segment | Profile | Primary Need | Channel |
|---|---|---|---|
| The Starter | 18-28, entry-level income | Access, simplicity | Mobile-first |
| The Builder | 28-45, growing income, family | Protection, savings, credit | Mobile + email |
| The Accumulator | 40-60, peak earning years | Growth, tax efficiency | Email + advisor |
| The Preserver | 60+, fixed income | Preservation, income | Branch + phone |

### Wealth Management
| Segment | AUM Range | Profile | Primary Message |
|---|---|---|---|
| Mass Affluent | $250K-$1M | Professional earners, DIY-inclined | Tools + guidance |
| HNW | $1M-$10M | Business owners, executives | Holistic planning |
| UHNW | $10M+ | Multi-generational wealth | White-glove access |
| Inheritors | Varies | Received windfall, navigating transition | Trust + simplicity |

### SMB / Commercial
| Segment | Profile | Primary Need |
|---|---|---|
| Micro Business | 1-9 employees, owner-operator | Cash flow, simple banking |
| Growth SMB | 10-100 employees, scaling | Credit, treasury, payroll |
| Established Mid-Market | 100-500 employees, finance team | Capital markets, risk management |

### Institutional
| Segment | Profile |
|---|---|
| Asset Managers | Execution, custody, reporting infrastructure |
| Pension Funds | Liability-driven investing, income stability |
| Endowments & Foundations | Mission-aligned, ESG-sensitive, long-horizon |
| Family Offices | Bespoke, relationship-driven, multi-asset |

---

## Segmentation Standards

1. Every segment must include a size estimate.
2. Every segment must include a reachability assessment across owned, earned, and paid channels.
3. HNW/UHNW segments must note relationship-led vs. digital acquisition dynamics.
4. Segments targeting seniors (65+) must include elder financial protection compliance note.

---

## Activation Recommendations

For each segment, provide:
1. Top 2 owned channels for initial engagement
2. Top 1 paid channel for acquisition
3. Content format recommendation per channel
4. Nurture sequence length
5. Compliance routing note
